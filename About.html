About X-Edge

An independent platform for discovering, understanding, and exploring AI tools.

X-Edge was created to simplify the way people discover and evaluate AI tools. With the rapid growth of artificial intelligence, finding the right tools — without hype or confusion — has become increasingly difficult.

X-Edge aims to solve this by curating and organizing AI tools in a clear, accessible, and transparent way.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make AI tools easier to discover and understand — especially for students, creators, developers, and early-stage founders.

We focus on clarity over promotion, usefulness over trends, and transparency over exaggeration.

What X-Edge Is (and Isn’t)

X-Edge is an independent discovery platform. We list and organize third-party tools for informational and educational purposes.

We are not:
• An official marketplace
• Affiliated with the tools listed
• A reseller or representative of any company

All trademarks, logos, and product names belong to their respective owners.

How We Handle Data

X-Edge collects minimal data. Currently, this includes email addresses only when users voluntarily submit them (for updates or announcements).

Email data is stored securely using Firebase (Google Cloud infrastructure), and we do not sell or share personal information.

For full details, please review our Privacy Policy.

Security & Transparency

X-Edge is built as a lightweight, static platform to minimize security risks. We prioritize safe defaults, minimal data collection, and clear communication.

As the platform evolves, security and privacy practices will evolve with it.

Project Status

X-Edge is currently an early-stage project (MVP). Features, design, and content may change as we learn from users and improve the platform.
